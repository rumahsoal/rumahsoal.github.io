5. language feature of motivation letter​


Jawaban:

Your motivation letter should be factual, concise, and firm. Flowery language does not necessarily fare well amongst the selection committee as they are reading your letter to get to know you, not how beautiful the sky was when you won your first gold medal.

A motivation letter is a written document that is used to express one's interest in a particular job or position. It is often written to a potential employer, and its primary purpose is to convince the employer that the writer is the best candidate for the job. The motivation letter often contains information about the writer's background, skills, and experience, as well as an explanation of why the writer is interested in the position and why he or she thinks he or she would be a good fit for the role.

Here are some key language features that you might encounter in a motivation letter:

1. Introduction: The introduction typically begins with a professional greeting and the writer's full name, address, and contact information.
2. Personalization: The letter should be customized to the specific job or position. It should address the potential employer by name and demonstrate a good understanding of their specific needs and requirements.
3. The writer's skills and experience: This section should describe the writer's background and qualifications in a way that is relevant to the job or position. This section may include information about the writer's education, past experience in similar roles, or any special skills or certificates he or she may have.
4. The writer's interest in the position: This section should explain why the writer is interested in the position and how he or she would benefit from the experience. This section may also discuss any personal or professional goals the writer has that the position could help him or her achieve.
5. The writer's enthusiasm: This section should demonstrate the writer's enthusiasm for the position. It may include examples of times the writer has gone above and beyond in previous roles, or it may explain why the writer is particularly excited about working for the potential employer.
6. Closure: The letter should end with a thank-you note and a confirmation of the writer's interest in the position. It should include any relevant details about how the employer can contact the writer for an interview or further questions.

In conclusion, a motivation letter is an effective way to demonstrate one's interest and qualifications for a particular job or position. By using clear and concise language, and by emphasizing the writer's enthusiasm and qualifications, a successful motivation letter can increase the writer's chances of being selected for an interview.

9. Jelaskan tentang motivation


Jawaban:

Motivasi berasal dari bahasa Latin "movere", yang berarti menggerakkan. Menurut Weiner (1990) motivasi didefenisikan sebagai kondisi internal yang membangkitkan kita untuk bertindak, mendorong kita mencapai tujuan tertentu, dan membuat kita tetap tertarik dalam kegiatan tertentu. Menurut Uno (2007), motivasi dapat diartikan sebagai dorongan internal dan eksternal dalam diri seseorang yang diindikasikan dengan adanya hasrat dan minat, dorongan dan kebutuhan, harapan dan cita-cita, penghargaan, dan penghormatan. Sedangkan Imron (1966) menjelaskan bahwa motivasi berasal dari bahasa Inggris "motivation" yang berarti dorongan atau pengalasan untuk melakukan suatu aktivitas hingga mencapai tujuan.

Dari serangkain pengertian para ahli di atas, maka dapat disimpulkan bahwa motivasi adalah sesuatu alasan yang mendorong seseorang untuk melakukan; menyelesaikan; menghentikan; dsb, suatu aktivitas guna mencapai tujuan tertentu yang diinginkan dari motivasi tersebut.

20. Contoh application letter​


Penjelasan:

Job application letter

atau surat lamaran pekerjaan merupakan surat yang dituliskan ketika kita ingin melamar pekerjaan untuk posisi/jabatan tertentu dalam suatu lowongan pekerjaan. Surat lamaran pekerjaan dalam bahasa Inggris terdiri atas 3 bagian besar, yaitu:

Salutation (pembukaan) adalah bagian pembukaan kepada siapa surat tersebut ditujukan (Umumnya pihak human resource department/HRD, kepala manajer, dll). Selalu gunakan format: Dear sir/madam (Jika nama serta jenis kelamin tidak diketahui) Dear Mr./Mrs./Ms. Last name (jika nama serta jenis kelamin diketahui)

Body of paragraph (isi)

Body atau isi adalah bagian inti dari cover letter. Pada bagian ini biasanya dituliskan latar belakang singkat tentang pendidikan dan pengalaman kerja yang menonjol, darimana informasi lowongan kerja diperoleh. Dan bisa juga mencantumkan kelengkapan data yang disertakan dalam amplop/e-mail, seperti curriculum vitae (CV) dan portofolio.

Closing (penutup) adalah bagian penutup dan pertanda akhir dari surat.

Dear Admissions Committee,

I had the pleasure of teaching Sara in her 11th grade honors English class at Mark Twain High School. From the first day of class, Sara impressed me with her ability to be articulate about difficult concepts and texts, her sensitivity to the nuances within literature, and her passion for reading, writing, and creative expression- both in and out of the classroom. Sara is a talented literary critic and poet, and she has my highest recommendation as a student and writer.

Sara is talented at considering the subleties within literature and the purpose behind authors' works. She produced an extraordinary year-long thesis paper on creative identity development, in which she compared works from three different time periods and synthesized cultural and historical perspectives to inform her analysis. When called upon to give her thesis defense in front of her peers, Sara spoke clearly and eloquently about her conclusions and responded to questions in a thoughtful way. Outside of the classroom, Sara is dedicated to her literary pursuits, especially to poetry. She publishes her poetry in our school's literary magazine, as well as in online magazines. She is an insightful, sensitive, and deeply self-aware individual driven to explore art, writing, and a deeper understanding of the human condition.

Throughout the year Sara was an active participant in our discussions, and she always supported her peers. Her caring nature and personality allow her to work well with others in a team setting, as she always respects others' opinions even when they differ from her own. When we held a class debate about gun laws, Sara opted to speak for the side opposite her own views. She explained her choice as motivated by a desire to put herself in other people's shoes, view the issues from a new perspective, and gain a clearer sense of the issue from all angles. Throughout the year, Sara demonstrated this openness to and empathy for the opinions, feelings, and perspectives of others, along with shrewd powers of observation, all qualities that makes her outstanding as a student of literature and burgeoning writer.

I am certain that Sara is going to continue to do great and creative things in her future. I highly recommend her for admission to your undergraduate program. She is talented, caring, intuitive, dedicated, and focused in her pursuits. Sara consistently seeks out constructive feedback so she can improve her writing skills, which is a rare and impressive quality in a high school student. Sara is truly a stand-out individual who will impress everyone she meets. Please feel free to contact me if you have any questions at callmeclemens@gmail.com.

Sincerely,

Ms. Scribe
English Teacher
Mark Twain High School
