Kalimat passive (passive voice)

Passive voice merupakan suatu bentuk kalimat dimana subjek kalimat menerima aksi, bukan melakukan aksi. Tidak seperti active voice yang fokus terhadap pihak yang melakukan aksi (doer of action), bentuk ini lebih berfokus kepada pihak atau objek yang menerima hasil dari suatu aksi tersebut (receiver of action).

Kalimat aktif yang bisa dirubah menjadi kalimat pasif berbentuk kalimat aktif yang mengandung transitive verbs (kata kerja yang memerlukan objek langsung) seperti make, bring, buy, write dan sebagainya. Sebaliknya, kalimat pasif yang mengandung intransitive verbs (kata kerja yang tidak memerlukan objek langsung) seperti cry, swim, go, arrive, dan die tidak bisa diubah menjadi kalimat pasif. Tetapi, apabila intransitive verbs tersebut diikuti oleh preposition, ada kemungkinan kalimat tersebut bisa diubah menjadi kalimat pasif tetapi kalimat pasif yang terbentuk akan sangat kaku.

Rumus Kalimat Pasif ( Passive Voice)

Subject(object dari kalimat aktif) + to be ( disesuaikan dengan bentuk tensesnya + Verb 3 (past participle)

TENSES                                              RUMUS PASSIVE VOICE

Simple present                                subject + is/am/are + Verb 3

Present continuous                     subject + is/am/are + being + Verb 3

Present perfect                              subject + have/has been + Verb 3

Simple past                                     subject + was/were + Verb 3

Past continuous                         subject + was/were + being + Verb 3

Past perfect                                     subject + had been + Verb 3

Simple future                                   subject + will be + Verb 3

Be going to                              subject + is/am/are + going to + be + Verb 3

Future perfect                                subject + will have been + Verb 3

Note : Penggunaan kalimat pasif dengan perfect continuous tenses biasanya jarang dilakukan karena akan menghasilkan struktur kalimat yang terlalu rumit dan sulit dipahami. Oleh karena itu, hal ini sebaiknya dihindari meskipun secara tata bahasa benar (grammatically correct).

Langkah-langkah (tips) membentuk kalimat pasif (Passive Voice) dari kalimat aktif:

1. Pilih objek penderita dari kalimat aktif itu

2. Buatlah objek tersebut menjadi subjek dari kalimat pasifnya

3. Perhatiakan bentuk tensesnya, subjeknya tunggal atau jamak karena tenses yang di gunakan harus sama

4. bentuklah predikatnya dengan to be + Verb 3

2. case about the implementation of national examination​


Penjelasan:

Certainly! Implementing a national examination system can have both advantages and disadvantages. Let's explore a case for and against the implementation of a national examination system:

**Case For Implementing a National Examination System:**

**1. Standardized Assessment:** National examinations ensure that all students are evaluated using the same criteria, which promotes fairness and uniformity in the assessment process.

**2. Quality Control:** They can serve as a quality control measure for education systems by setting minimum standards that schools and teachers must meet.

**3. Accountability:** National exams can hold schools and teachers accountable for the quality of education they provide. This can incentivize educators to strive for higher standards.

**4. College and Career Opportunities:** Standardized exams can simplify the college admissions process, making it easier for institutions to evaluate applicants from diverse educational backgrounds.

**Case Against Implementing a National Examination System:**

**1. Overemphasis on Testing:** An overreliance on standardized testing can lead to a narrow curriculum, with teachers "teaching to the test" and neglecting other important aspects of education.

**2. Stress and Pressure:** High-stakes national exams can create significant stress and pressure on students, potentially leading to mental health issues.

**3. Inequality:** Some students may not have equal access to resources and preparation for these exams, leading to disparities in performance and opportunities.

**4. Creativity and Critical Thinking:** Standardized exams often prioritize rote memorization over critical thinking and creativity, which are essential skills in the modern world.

**Conclusion:** The implementation of a national examination system should be carefully considered, balancing the benefits of standardized assessment with the potential drawbacks. Policymakers must address issues such as curriculum balance, equity in access to resources, and the well-being of students to ensure that the system serves the best interests of education and society.

17. 10 A Mathematics examination consists of Papers I, II and III. The full score of each paper is 100. In order to pass the examination, the average score of all three papers must be at least 60. A student scores 57 marks in Paper I. (a) Ifx is the average score of Paper II and Paper III, write down an inequality in x such that the student passes the examination. (b) Hence solve the inequality to find the minimum value of x.


Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

(a) To pass the examination, the average score of all three papers must be at least 60. Let's assume the average score of Paper II and Paper III as x.

The average score of the three papers can be calculated as (57 + x) / 3.

To pass the examination, this average score must be greater than or equal to 60. Therefore, we can write the inequality:

(57 + x) / 3 ≥ 60

(b) To solve the inequality and find the minimum value of x, we can follow these steps:

Multiply both sides of the inequality by 3 to eliminate the denominator:

57 + x ≥ 180

Subtract 57 from both sides of the inequality:

x ≥ 180 - 57

x ≥ 123

Therefore, the minimum value of x is 123. This means the student needs to score at least 123 on average in Paper II and Paper III to pass the examination.

38. Profil lembaga pendidikan sekolah masuk penelitian apa


Jawaban:

Profil lembaga pendidikan sekolah dapat menjadi subjek penelitian dalam berbagai bidang, seperti pendidikan, psikologi, sosiologi, dan ekonomi. Beberapa contoh topik penelitian yang dapat dilakukan terkait dengan profil lembaga pendidikan sekolah antara lain:

1. Analisis kinerja dan efektivitas lembaga pendidikan sekolah, misalnya melalui pengukuran tingkat kelulusan siswa, tingkat keterlibatan siswa dalam kegiatan sekolah, dan hasil ujian atau penilaian lainnya.

2. Studi tentang karakteristik siswa dan pengaruhnya terhadap hasil belajar, seperti kemampuan akademik, bakat, minat, dan keteraturan dalam belajar.

3. Penelitian tentang pola pengajaran, metode pengajaran, dan kualitas pengajaran, termasuk penggunaan teknologi dalam pembelajaran.

4. Analisis kebijakan dan manajemen lembaga pendidikan sekolah, termasuk pengelolaan sumber daya, kebijakan penerimaan siswa, dan pembuatan anggaran.

5. Studi tentang peran lembaga pendidikan sekolah dalam masyarakat, misalnya keterkaitannya dengan dunia kerja dan kontribusinya terhadap pengembangan masyarakat setempat.

Penjelasan:

Dalam penelitian tentang profil lembaga pendidikan sekolah, biasanya menggunakan berbagai metode pengumpulan data, seperti wawancara, kuesioner, pengamatan langsung, dan analisis dokumen. Data yang dikumpulkan kemudian dianalisis menggunakan teknik statistik dan metodologi penelitian lainnya untuk mendapatkan temuan yang signifikan dan bermanfaat.
