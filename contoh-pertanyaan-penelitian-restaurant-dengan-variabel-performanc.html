Bentuk Simple Present :

Dengan main action :

(+) Subject + Verb 1 + Object + ......

(-) Subject + Do/Does + Not + Verb 1 + Object + ...

(?) Do/Does + Subject + Verb 1 + Object + .......

Misalnya,

(+) She wants you to join the party with me today

(-) She doesn't want to join the party with me today

(?) Does she want to join the party with me today?

20. contoh dialog present perfect tense 4 orng, bertema kitchen/restaurant


Jawaban:

Berikut adalah contoh dialog present perfect tense 4 orang dengan tema kitchen/restaurant:

A: Have you ever cooked Indonesian food before?

B: Yes, I have. I've cooked rendang and nasi goreng.

C: Wow, that's impressive. I've never cooked Indonesian food before.

D: I have, but I've only made gado-gado. It's my favorite dish.

A: Has anyone tried the new restaurant downtown?

B: Yes, I have. The food was delicious.

C: I haven't tried it yet. What did you order?

B: I ordered the chicken satay and the fried rice.

D: I haven't tried it either. Maybe we can go there together next time.

A: Who has cleaned the kitchen today?

B: I have. I washed the dishes and wiped the counters.

C: I haven't cleaned the kitchen yet. I'll do it later.

D: I cleaned the kitchen yesterday, so it's not my turn today.

A: Have you ever baked a cake from scratch?

B: Yes, I have. I've made chocolate cake and carrot cake.

C: I haven't baked a cake from scratch before. It seems difficult.

D: I have, but I've only made simple cakes like vanilla and lemon.

Dalam dialog di atas, present perfect tense digunakan untuk menyatakan pengalaman masa lalu yang masih berhubungan dengan masa sekarang. Contoh kalimat present perfect tense yang digunakan dalam dialog tersebut antara lain "I have cooked rendang and nasi goreng", "I have tried the new restaurant downtown", "I have cleaned the kitchen today", dan "I have made chocolate cake and carrot cake".
