20. Cara membuat puding coklat dengan step dalam bahasa inggris


Chocolate Pudding Ingredients :

1 Packet Gelatin Powder

150 G Granulated Sugar

1/2 Teaspoon Salt

1/2 Can Of Sweetened Condensed Milk Chocolate

1 Tablespoons Chocolate Powder

1 Liter Of Water To Cook (Pakek Fresh Milk Can Also Be)

Vla Sauce Ingredients:

1 Can Evaporated Milk

200 Ml Fresh Milk

4 Tablespoons Granulated Sugar

2 Teaspoons Flour Custard.

1/2 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract

2 Teaspoons Cornflour

How To Easily Make Chocolate Pudding

A Rather Large Sized Bowl, Put All The Chocolate Pudding Ingredients (Except Sugar And Salt), Stir Until Well Blended And Into Solution, Then Filter. After That, Pour Into The Pan.

Add Granulated Sugar And Salt, Then Mix Until Well Blended. Cook Until Boiling On Medium Heat While Stirring Occasionally. Lift And Let Stand Until Steam Heat To Dissipate.

Pour A Solution Of Chocolate Pudding Into The Mold That Has Been Moistened With A Little Water Before.For Mold/Loyangnya To Suit Your Taste.

After That, Enter The Mold Baking Pan Containing The Pudding Into The Fridge And Wait Until The Pudding Is Really Hard.

For The Sauce Vlanya, Put All The Ingredients (Except Sugar) Into A Different Container. Mix Until Well Blended And Slick, Then Filter.

Put In A Saucepan, Add Sugar, And Cook Until It Explodes–Letup And Thickened. Remove From The Stove And Let The Sauce Cool Vlanya First.

31. pertanyaan di atas Apakah cetakan itu masih bisa diisi puding coklat lagibisa tidak bisa ​


Jawab:

Diketahui

a) Dimensi cetakan balok :

Panjang : 15cm

Lebar : 15cm

Tinggi : 20cm

b) Dimensi gelas ukur (balok)

Panjang : 10 cm

Lebar : 10 cm

Tinggi : 15 cm

c) Jumlah penuangan (n) = 2 kali gelas ukur

Ditanya :

Apakah cetakan masih bisa diisi puding coklat lagi?

Penyelesaian :

a) Mengukur volume cetakan balok

Volume balok = Panjang x Lebar x Tinggi

Volume balok = 15cm x 15cm x 20 cm

Volume balok = 4500cm³

b) Mengukur volume gelas ukur balok

Volume balok (2) = Panjang x lebar x tinggi

Volume balok (2) = 10cm x 10cm x 15cm

Volume balok (2) = 1500cm³

c) Mengukur volume sisa

= Volume balok - (n x volume balok (2))

= 4500cm³ - (2 x 1500cm³)

= 4500cm³-3000cm³

= 500cm³

Jadi karena terdapat sekitar 500cm³ volume cetakan balok yang masih kosong, raina masih dapat mengisi puding coklat ke dalam cetakan tersebut hingga penuh.

36. buatlah langkah langkah dalam membuat puding coklat dalam bahasa Inggris​


Ingredients

2 Large Egg Yolks

2 tbsp. Cornstarch

2 c. Whole Milk, Divided

1/2 c. Sugar

1/4 c. Unsweetened Cocoa Powder

1/4 tsp. Salt

2 tsp. Chocolate Extract (or Vanilla Extract)

STEPS :

1. In a medium bowl, whisk the egg yolks for about 60 seconds until light yellow and increased in volume, then whisk in the cornstarch and about 1/4 cup of the milk. Once smooth and incorporated, set aside.

2. Place the sugar, cocoa, salt, and remaining milk in a saucepan, and bring to a scald over medium-high heat, which is when the liquid is about 180-190ºF. This is before the mixture comes to a boil, and in this stage you will see little bubbles start forming on the sides of the pan. Remove the pan from the heat and, while whisking constantly, dribble the hot cocoa into the egg cornstarch mixture very gradually. We are tempering so we don't scramble the eggs. Pour everything back into the saucepan and cook over medium high heat, whisking constantly, until the pudding comes to a full boil. Reduce the heat to a simmer and continue whisking for a couple minutes until the pudding is thickened.

3. Remove the pudding from the heat and stir in the chocolate or vanilla extract. Pour the pudding into a bowl and press plastic wrap directly on the surface of the pudding so a skin doesn't form. Refrigerate for an hour or two until chilled.

SERVE & ENJOY
