Research Methods/Types of Research
< Research Methods
Research can be classified in many different ways on the basis of the methodology of research, the knowledge it creates, the user group, the research problem it investigates etc.


A research design according to Andrew B kirumbi (2018) is the set of methods and procedures used in collecting and analyzing measures of the variables specified in the research problem research. The design of a study defines the study type (descriptive, correlation, semi-experimental, experimental, review, meta-analytic) and sub-type (e.g., descriptive-longitudinal case study), research problem, hypotheses, independent and dependent variables, experimental design, and, if applicable, data collection methods and a statistical analysis plan. Research design is the framework that has been created to find answers to research questions.

Seorang mekanik baru yang bekerja di sebuah perusahaan otomotif nasional sering mendapat teguran dari atasan karena resume hasil pekerjaannya berkali-kali tidak lolos tahap QC (Quality Control). Mekanik tersebut ingin memperbaiki kinerjanya, dengan pertama kali mengindentifikasi kekurangannya dengan cara mengindentifikasi sendiri kemungkinan kesalahan yang ia buat dan menanyakan kepada rekan sekerja terhadap kekurangan atau kesalahan. Berdasarkan informasi yang diperoleh mekanik tersebut kemudian mencari solusi yang tepat dengan cara berkonsultasi dengan rekan sekerja, menambah ilmu dengan membaca buku terkait, terus mengasah keterampilannya dan lain-lain. Selanjutnya si mekanik harus menentukan beberapa langkah tindakan untuk mengatasi dan memperbaiki kemampuannya (planning). Kemudian, mekanik itu harus menerapkan langkah-langkah yang telah direncanakan tersebut dalam beberapa kali kerja (tindakan). Selama melakukan perbaikan kinerja tersebut, si mekanik harus selalu mengamati hasil kerjanya sendiri dan mendiskusikannya dengan rekan sekerja yang dianggap mumpuni serta pihak QC (pengamatan). Dan berikutnya si mekanik harus melakukan analisis hasil dari program aksi yang telah dia canangkan tersebut (refleksi). Jika berhasil ia teruskan, jika masih kurang berhasil berarti ia harus menemukan cara atau strategi lain.

Jawaban:

First of all, don't forget to include the place and date of the letter. This may seem trivial, but by including the date, you will be considered that you are serious about looking for work. If there is no date, month and year, the company's HRD might consider your job application letter to be an old job application letter.

Next, write the destination address for your job application letter. The company name and address must be written completely and correctly.

In the opening section, state where you got the job information. Whether from newspapers, social media, websites or recommended by someone. Also state the job position you would like the company to open for job vacancies in various positions.

Write down your personal data, including your full name, place of birth, last education, address, email (if any) and telephone number.

Mention all attached files that you include in the job application letter. For example, recent photographs, photocopies of ID cards, curriculum vitae, photocopies of recent diplomas, paklaring (work experience letters), photocopies of course certificates or work training that you have attended, yellow letters, SKCK, and so on.

Mention your work experience, and the abilities you have that could support the job position you want.

At the end, show that you are enthusiastic about the job you are applying for and have a strong desire to occupy the job position.

After writing the thank you notes and greetings, don't forget to sign your job application letter.

In writing the job application letter, it is mandatory to use good and correct Indonesian according to EYD (Enhanced Spelling). Don't use slang, let alone Alay. For letters use a formal typeface, for example, Times New Roman or Arial.

Write a job application letter that is solid, clear and polite. Don't be convoluted. There is no need to be long-winded, for example explaining all educational history starting from kindergarten and early childhood education. You don't need to also write down all your hobbies and achievements, especially if it's not related to the type of job you are going to apply for.

Write your own job application letter. Do not plagiarize or completely copy job application letters circulating on the internet. You can imitate, but don't trace it whole, change it a little with your own words. Usually companies are very familiar with job application letters that are only copy and paste from the internet. And if that is the case, the HRD will immediately get rid of applications like that, because the applicant is considered lazy and not creative.

If the application letter includes an email, or if the application letter will be sent via email, avoid using the same email address. For example agungcuteabis@gmail.com or susinaqmanjah@gmail.com. Email looks very unprofessional. Use a kind and humane email address such as agungsunarko@gmail.com or susirosmala@gmail.com.

Include photos that are formal and polite, wearing a shirt or coat with a blue or red background, like the photos on the KTP. Don't use selfies with scenic backgrounds.

Do not write down your experience of having worked at a company, where you stopped working at that company because you were fired, especially if you were fired because of a fatal mistake. This makes prospective employers afraid to accept you. Worried that you might make the same mistake at his company.

Also avoid writing work experiences, but only briefly you worked at the company. This can make you feel like a boring, loyal person.

If the job application letter is handwritten, write it clearly and neatly. Don't be like a doctor's writing that is hard to read. No abbreviations. Use a ballpoint pen (pen / pen) in black or blue ink.

19. buatlah UX research plan untuk satu produk yang memiliki mobile application seperti Twitter


Jawaban:

Berikut adalah contoh rencana penelitian pengalaman pengguna (UX Research) untuk produk dengan aplikasi perangkat seluler serupa dengan Twitter:

Tujuan Penelitian:

- Memahami kebutuhan dan preferensi pengguna dalam menggunakan aplikasi perangkat seluler serupa dengan Twitter.

- Mempelajari tingkat kepuasan pengguna terhadap fitur dan fungsionalitas yang ada.

- Mengidentifikasi masalah dan potensi perbaikan dalam pengalaman pengguna.

Metodologi Penelitian:

1. Wawancara Pengguna:

- Melakukan wawancara dengan beberapa pengguna aktif aplikasi perangkat seluler serupa dengan Twitter.

- Menanyakan tentang pengalaman mereka dalam menggunakan aplikasi, fitur yang paling disukai, masalah yang mereka hadapi, dan saran perbaikan.

2. Observasi Pengguna:

- Mengamati pengguna saat menggunakan aplikasi pada perangkat seluler mereka.

- Memperhatikan interaksi pengguna dengan fitur-fitur utama, navigasi, dan antarmuka pengguna.

3. Penilaian Heuristik:

- Melakukan evaluasi aplikasi berdasarkan prinsip-prinsip desain yang telah ditetapkan.

- Menilai kesesuaian aplikasi dengan prinsip-prinsip desain, seperti ketersediaan informasi, konsistensi antarmuka, dan kesesuaian dengan kebutuhan pengguna.

4. A/B Testing:

- Mengadakan eksperimen dengan menggunakan dua versi aplikasi yang berbeda.

- Mengumpulkan data dari pengguna yang menggunakan kedua versi aplikasi.

- Menganalisis data dan membandingkan efektivitas dan kepuasan pengguna antara kedua versi.

5. Survei Kepuasan Pengguna:

- Menyebarkan survei kepada pengguna untuk mengukur tingkat kepuasan mereka terhadap aplikasi.

- Mengumpulkan umpan balik tentang fitur-fitur yang paling disukai dan perbaikan yang diinginkan.

Analisis Data dan Temuan:

- Menganalisis data yang diperoleh dari wawancara, observasi, penilaian heuristik, A/B testing, dan survei kepuasan pengguna.

- Mengidentifikasi dan merangkum temuan utama seputar kebutuhan pengguna, masalah yang ada, dan saran perbaikan.

- Membuat rekomendasi perbaikan dan perubahan pada fitur dan fungsi aplikasi berdasarkan temuan penelitian.

Implementasi Perubahan:

- Menerapkan rekomendasi perbaikan dan perubahan yang ditemukan dalam penelitian UX ke dalam aplikasi.

- Melakukan pengujian lanjutan setelah perubahan diterapkan untuk memeriksa efektivitasnya dan mengukur perubahan kepuasan pengguna.

Permulaan:

- Mengawasi penggunaan aplikasi dan mendengarkan umpan balik pengguna secara rutin.

- Menggunakan temuan dan rekomendasi dari penelitian ini untuk menginformasikan pengembangan dan perbaikan selanjutnya.

Rencana ini dapat disesuaikan dengan kebutuhan dan ruang lingkup spesifik dari aplikasi perangkat seluler yang serupa dengan Twitter.

32. Write some examples of sociolinguistics research!​


Language attitudes and stereotypes: Sociolinguists may investigate how individuals perceive and evaluate different language varieties and the social implications associated with those attitudes. For example, a study could explore the perception of accents in hiring practices or how linguistic prejudices affect social interactions.Language and identity: Sociolinguists often examine how language use reflects and shapes individual and group identities. They may study language choices among multilingual speakers and investigate how language plays a role in constructing social identities, such as gender, ethnicity, or social class.Language variation and change: Sociolinguists investigate how language varies within a community or across different communities. They may explore regional dialects, urban vernaculars, or the use of different language varieties based on social contexts. Research might involve analyzing linguistic features, such as pronunciation, vocabulary, or grammatical structures, and studying their distribution and evolution.Language and power: Sociolinguistic research often examines how language is used to exert power and control within society. This can include studying language policies, language planning, or the role of language in institutional settings, such as education, law, or media. Researchers might investigate linguistic inequalities and how language can be a tool for social, economic, or political dominance.Language and social interaction: Sociolinguists analyze the ways in which language is used in everyday communication and social interactions. They might study conversational patterns, speech acts, politeness strategies, or the negotiation of meaning in different contexts. This research can provide insights into how language reflects and shapes social relationships and social dynamics.Language and migration: Sociolinguists may focus on language use and acquisition among immigrant communities. They might explore language maintenance and shift, bilingualism, language contact phenomena, and the impact of language on the integration and identity formation of migrants and their descendants.Language and technology: With the rise of digital communication platforms and social media, sociolinguistic research has also expanded to examine how language is used and influenced by technology. This can involve studying online language practices, computer-mediated communication, text messaging, or the impact of technology on language change and variation.Language policy and planning: Sociolinguists often engage in research on language policies at national, regional, or local levels. This can include investigating language revitalization efforts, language endangerment, language rights, or language-in-education policies. Researchers may explore the sociopolitical factors that shape language policy decisions and their consequences for linguistic diversity and social inclusion.

These examples illustrate the broad range of topics within sociolinguistics, and researchers employ various methodologies, including surveys, interviews, ethnography, corpus analysis, and quantitative techniques, to investigate these areas of inquiry.

38. Contoh grounded research


Grounded Research atau Grounded Theory merupakan sebuah metode yang tergolong baru dalam ilmu sosial. Metode ini pertama kali dikenalkan pada cabang ilmu sosiologi oleh Glasser dan Strauss dalam bukunya berjudul The Discovery of Grounded Theory pada tahun 1967. Metode ini kemudian lebih lanjut dikembangkan oleh Schlegel.

contoh:

Cliffort Geertz pernah membangun teori tentang masyarakat muslim Jawa yang terbagi menjadi kelas priyayi, santri dan abangan. Ketika saat ini kita ingin melakukan penelitian grounded tentang masyarakat muslim di suatu desa di Jawa, kita tidak perlu menerapkan klasifikasi yang dibuat oleh Geertz tersebut. Melainkan, kita harus melihat data lalu memunculkan konsep sebagaimana data tersebut berbicara.

Sangat mungkin klasifikasi yang dicetus oleh Geertz tidak relevan untuk situasi sosial yang khas dari masyarakat yang kita teliti, bahkan sekalipun kita melakukan penelitian di desa yang sama dengan yang diteliti Geertz. Hal ini karena situasi sosial bersifat dinamis, berkembang, dan terus berubah.
